COVID


The practice of Aikido entails a partner. Hence in order to ensure the safety of all, a strict sanitary protocol is implemented:

  • - Complete vaccination is compulsory (QR code will be scanned during your first class).
  • - Mask is compulsory inside the Dojo (including during classes).
  • - Sanitizartion of hands before and after each class.
  • - Bring a clean keiko gi for each class.
  • - You will need to stop the practice and take a PCR test in case of any symptom of after any risky situation.
  • - Limited number of students in changing rooms.

Registration


If you wish to discover Aikido, you are welcome to try one or two classes. Please make sure to read the page FUNCTIONING OF THE DOJO prior to your arrival.

Obligation: a 36€ cheque just for the duration of the trial.

We ask for a cheque deposit written out to the FFAB for Federal Insurance – in order to be insured in case of a problem. It is naturally returned if the trial class is not conclusive.

Trial will not be allowed without deposit of this cheque.

Please consult the COVID section.

In order to facilitate the organization of classes, please present yourself at least 15min before class hours for registration.

Visitors


People regularly practicing in another dojo may train as visitors. If you wish to participate in training, please email us at the dojo address (see above). Please present your dojo membership book and/or insurance when you come to train.

The daily fee for visitors is 10€ / day.
