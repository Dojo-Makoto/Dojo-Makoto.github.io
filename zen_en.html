Secular Meditation


Meditation is an opportunity for experiencing intimacy with oneself: sitting, paying attention to one's posture and breath, standing still and doing nothing but being aware of the present moment.


Schedules


During the COVID period, meditation is integrated at the beginning of some of the Aikido classes.

Please present yourself 15min before class. During meditation, the Dojo will be closed.

Wear dark and comfortable cloth. Bring zoris (sandals).

If you have a zafu, please bring it.
